POPULATION

    Snow leopards usually live alone, but only in pairs around the rutting season.

  • 2

    They usually have fixed nests, located in rocky caves, rocky hollows, rocky crevices or bushes under rocks, mostly on sunny slopes, and often do not leave a nest for years.
